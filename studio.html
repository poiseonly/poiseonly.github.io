THE STUDIO

spaces that stay with you

29th Avenue Designs is a full-spectrum interior design studio based in Lagos, Nigeria, rooted in the belief that a well-designed space doesn’t just look good, it feels like something. It feels like you.

We design with intention. Every material chosen, every corner considered, every detail deliberate. Because the spaces you live and work in shape how you feel every single day, and that is not something we take lightly.

At 29th Avenue Designs, we go beyond aesthetics. We take time to understand who you are, how you move through a space, and what you need to truly feel at home in it. We listen first. Then we create. And what we create is an experience that is entirely, specifically yours. No two spaces are the same. Neither are the people in them.
