OUR PROCESS

from consultation to creation

Step 01
Consultation

Our process starts with you scheduling a consultation, where we come to your home and spend time with you. Timing depends on the scope of your space. This is the first important step. We will start with a tour of your space. We want to understand how you want to live in your space, as we believe spaces are personal.

We listen to get an idea of what your design style is (if there is one) and explore possibilities of how to use your space.

Many clients give blank canvases, in which case there is nothing existing to work with. Some clients might have a treasured piece of art, for example, or furniture that they may want to keep and use in any room. It's always good to know every detail upfront.

Step 02
Site Analysis

We have an entire image library broken up into different design styles. Looking through all these images allows us to do a style analysis with you. We need to figure out what look and feel you are drawn to, what you love in the images and what elements in them you like.

We invite you to be as honest as possible as this will help us build a whole combined profile of the type of design that would suit your taste.

Step 03
The Walk Through

We will have a little walk through in the rooms of your home that you want us to work on. We will make a list of every item we will bring into the room. This is done together with you, of course, because we will be asking you what you want to use each room for.

Making the wishlist of items helps us identify what sort of budget you will need to spend on products. That is pretty much what we cover in the walk through.

You are absolutely free to pick our brain on anything and everything design during this time.

We will also be suggesting things as they come to mind walking through each space. It might be removing a non-load bearing wall to open up the floor plan, or rearranging the furniture to maximise the flow — basically something we could do to improve the space. You name it.

Step 04
The After Planning

Once we leave your space, we will send an email. The email will contain everything we discussed during our visit consultation. It will also list the amount of money we estimate you will spend on the project products for your projects.

The timelines on the projects helps determine what our design charge will be.

Next Steps: After planning, we'll present you with mood boards, material samples, and a detailed project timeline.

Ready to start your journey?

Every space tells a story. Let us help you tell yours.
