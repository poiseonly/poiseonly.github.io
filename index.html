THE STUDIO where magic happens

24th Avenue is a multi-disciplinary design studio based in Lagos, Nigeria that focuses on interior design and everyday objects through storytelling and re-imagination. We are committed to showing people that there are infinite ways to design spaces that feel like home.

Every space you encounter should be an experience. Here at 24th Avenue, we dare to push the boundaries of creativity and challenge the rules. With empathy at the forefront of our design process, our interdisciplinary team works diligently to create monumental spaces that are reflective of what our clients envision.

THE FOUNDER visionary

Oluwasegun Adedamola

Founder & Principal Designer

Born in Ogun, Nigeria and raised in a traditional Nigerian household, Oluwasegun knew that he wanted to defy traditional expectations and pursue a creative career. He earned his bachelor's degree in industrial design with a minor in textile technology at North Carolina State University.

Oluwasegun launched his career as a successful color + material designer where he influenced footwear products for notable athletes and artists. However, in a multi-hyphenated generation, Oluwasegun knew he wanted to extend himself into other creative pursuits.

"He believes if God created the heavens + earth, it pleases him to push spaces + ideas He'll create through him. With an eye for detail, drawing inspiration from his experiences and the world around him, Oluwasegun works on instinct to push the boundaries of his creative limits."
