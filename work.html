Adedamola Samuel

Founder & Principal Designer

Adedamola Samuel is the founder of 29avenuedesigns. A graduate of Materials Engineering, Adedamola has always been fascinated by the way materials speak, how combining the right textures, finishes, and elements can transform something ordinary into something that resonates deeply and lingers long after you've left the room.

That fascination became a foundation. With a trained eye for detail and a natural instinct for storytelling, Adedamola approaches every space as a visual narrative, one where every element is intentional, every layer carries meaning, and every room tells a story worth experiencing. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, he brings a distinctly grounded and considered perspective to interior design, shaped by culture, curiosity, and a deep appreciation for the world around him.

He believes that intentional design is one of the most powerful ways we can honour the spaces we live in and that belief sits at the heart of everything 29avenuedesigns creates.
